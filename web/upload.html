Suggested Walkthrough

                    

                    
    
                        
  • Start with the seeded demo journeys to show the three healthcare use cases immediately.
    • 
                        
  • Click `Populate demo signals` to fill the queue with low, medium, and high urgency cases for review.

    • 
                        
  • Mark the patient page or spotlight case as reviewed to show the closure step, then return to the console and point to the updated queue plus the Tuner event timeline.
